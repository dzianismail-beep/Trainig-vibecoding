About Me

With over 15 years of experience in venture capital and private equity, I have dedicated my career to identifying and nurturing high-potential businesses across various industries. My passion lies in supporting entrepreneurs who are building innovative solutions to real-world problems.

I began my journey in finance at Goldman Sachs, where I spent 8 years in their investment banking division. This experience provided me with a deep understanding of financial markets and corporate strategy. In 2015, I transitioned to private investing, founding my own investment firm focused on early-stage technology companies.

My investment philosophy centers on backing exceptional founders who demonstrate strong vision, execution capability, and market understanding. I believe in being a hands-on investor, providing not just capital but also strategic guidance, industry connections, and operational expertise.

Beyond investing, I serve as a mentor to several startup accelerators and regularly speak at industry conferences about entrepreneurship and venture capital trends.
